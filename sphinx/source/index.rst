Welcome to the Bone Physics documentation!
===========================================

Bone Physics is a blender add-on focused on skeletal physics simulation for character animation. 
It is suitable for dynamic parts like hair, ribbons, tails, as well as soft-body parts

Check |youtube_link| or |bilibili_link| for video tutorials and complete workflow demonstrations.
